Mañana

Lamenatzeach el hanekhilot mizmor leDavid. Amrai haazina Adonai, biná hagígi. Hakshiva lekol shav'i, Malkí veElohai, ki eleja etpalel. Adonai, boker tishma koli; boker e'erkha lakh va'atzapeh. Ki lo El chafetz resha atah, lo yegurcha ra. Lo yit-yatzvu holelim le'negdekha, saneta kol po'alei aven. To'ved dovershaker, ish damim u'mirmah yetaev Adonai. Va'ani berov chasdekha avo veitecha, eshtachaveh el heichal kodshecha beyiratecha. Adonai, nechenei betzidkatecha lema'an shorerai; hayasher lefanai darkecha. Ki ein befihem nechonah, kirbam havot; kever patuach gronam, leshonam yachaliku. Ha'ashimem Elohim, yiplu mimotzoteihem; bedrov pish'ehem hadichemo, ki maru vach. Veyismekhu kol chosei vach, le'olam yeranenu, vesachot bam; veya'otzu vach ohavei shemecha. Ki atah tevarech tzadik, Adonai, katzina ratzon ta'atreinu.

Tarde

Mizmor shir chanukat habayit leDavid. Aromimkha Adonai ki dilitani, velo simachta oyvai li. Adonai Elohai, shiváti elecha vatirpa'eini. Adonai, he'elitá min sheol nafshi; chiyitani miyordei vor. Zameru lAdonai chasidav, vehodu lezécher kodsho. Ki rega beapo, chayim birtzono; ba'erev yalin bechi, velaboker rinah. Va'ani amarti beshalvi bal emot le'olam. Adonai birtzonekha he'emadeta lehareri oz; histarta fanecha hayiti nivhal. Elecha Adonai ekra, ve'el Adonai etchanan. Mah betza bedami, berideti el shachat? Hayodcha afar, hayagid amitecha? Shema Adonai vechoneneni; Adonai heyeh ozer li. Hafachta mispedi lemachol li, pitachta sakki vat'azreni simchah. Lemaan yezamerkha kavod velo yidom; Adonai Elohai le'olam odeka.

Noche

Mizmor letodah: Hariu lAdonai kol ha'aretz. Ivdu et Adonai besimchah, bo'u lefanav birnanah. De'u ki Adonai Hu Elohim; Hu asanu velo anachnu, amo vetzon mar'ito. Bo'u she'arav betodah, chatzerotav bitehilah; hodu lo, barechu shemo. Ki tov Adonai, le'olam chasdo, ve'ad dor vador emunato.
